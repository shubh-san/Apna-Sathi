●what is apna sathi?

Apna Sathi is a career guidance and counselling platform created to support students in making informed decisions about their education and future. The platform helps students understand their interests, choose the right career path, and follow a clear roadmap toward success. Through expert guidance, counselling support, and career-focused resources, Apna Sathi aims to reduce confusion, build confidence, and guide students toward achieving their academic and professional goals.
